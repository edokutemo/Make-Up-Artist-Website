Winnie Illona is a Batam based freelance makeup artist. Makeup, beauty and hairstyling has always been her passion.

Her journey started off as a student in Cosmoprof Academy (Singapore). She graduated from the makeup artist course with 'Distinction' on July 2016. Since then she has always practice her skill by doing pro-bono makeup.



Prior pursuing her career in makeup, Winnie has graduated with a Bachelor in Psychology from James Cook University Singapore.
